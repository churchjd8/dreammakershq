# LinkedIn Content Marketing Strategy — Jeff Church

## Overview

**Goal:** Establish Jeff Church as the go-to voice for CPG founders navigating the journey from launch to $100M+ exit. Drive awareness to CPG Founders Group (cpgfoundersgroup.com), build trust, and grow the community.

**Cadence:** 2x per week (Tuesday + Thursday)
**Start Date:** Week of April 21, 2026

---

## Content Mix

### Tuesday Posts — "The Lesson" (Story-Driven)
- 800-1,500 characters
- Built around a single real story with a real lesson
- Opens with a scene or moment, creates tension, draws out the principle
- Feels like Jeff telling you something over coffee
- Always includes specific names, numbers, or dates

### Thursday Posts — "Quick Hit" (Punchy Wisdom)
- 300-700 characters
- One principle or aphorism with 2-3 sentences of context
- Feels like a voice note Jeff recorded after a call with a founder
- Ends with a direct question, imperative, or one-liner
- More white space, shorter paragraphs, stacked lines

---

## Content Pillars (Rotate Through)

1. **Founder Mistakes** — Fatal flaws, avoidable traps, things that kill companies
2. **Suja War Stories** — Specific moments from building to $100M+ (board dynamics, Coca-Cola, operations)
3. **Operational Wisdom** — Margins, inventory, fundraising, hiring, retail — the tactical stuff
4. **Personal / Vulnerability** — Burnout, rehab, family cost, the human side. 2x/month max.
5. **Mentorship / Babu** — Advice he gives founders now, patterns he sees, what the next generation needs to hear

---

## Engagement Strategy

- **Daily:** 15 minutes of commenting on relevant CPG/founder/retail posts. Genuine engagement, not "great post!" — add a perspective, share a quick lesson, ask a question.
- **Weekly:** Reply to every comment on Jeff's posts within 24 hours. Start conversations.
- **Monthly:** 1 longer LinkedIn article or newsletter (repurposed from blog content on cpgfoundersgroup.com). Links back to the site.
- **Ongoing:** Repurpose every blog post into 2-3 LinkedIn posts. Repurpose podcast/meeting quotes into quick hits.

---

## Posting Guidelines (Voice)

- Should feel like a transcribed voice note, not a drafted Google Doc
- More ellipses, parentheticals, exclamation points
- Fewer em-dashes
- Let thoughts breathe as incomplete sentences
- No emojis
- Trust the reader — don't over-explain
- Use Jeff's natural aphorisms and signature phrases
- Specific names, numbers, dates — never vague
- Profanity is fine when authentic and the stakes are real
- Rougher edges > clean copy

---

## Success Metrics (Monthly Review)

- **Impressions per post** (target: 5,000+ by month 3)
- **Engagement rate** (target: 3%+ — likes + comments + reposts / impressions)
- **Profile views** (leading indicator of brand awareness)
- **Website clicks** to cpgfoundersgroup.com
- **DMs from founders** (qualitative — are the right people reaching out?)
- **Newsletter signups** attributed to LinkedIn
